About the Author

Sarthak Mishra is an emerging author who explores psychological and philosophical storytelling. His work examines logic, human nature, and the darker dimensions of the human mind.

Book

The Logic of Bones

The Logic of Bones follows a brilliant but disturbed mind where logic replaces empathy and emotions are seen as weakness. The story explores a psychological conflict between reason, morality, and the limits of human detachment.
